About the Game

Feeling abandoned, a doll comes to life to find out what happened to the little girl she used to play with. But her journey won't be easy! Through a forsaken doll's sorrowful eyes, will you be able to free yourself or remain forever shackled to your Anxiety Wires?

Features

- Explore the workshop where the doll was created, searching for traces in your past memories.

- Solve puzzles creatively to overcome your many fears.

- Immerse yourself in a scenery that evokes her memories with clever use of sepia, light effects, and more.
